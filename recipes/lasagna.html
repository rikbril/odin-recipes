Lasagna

Description

An delicous lasagna for four persons. half hour of prep and half an hour of oven time. this lasagna contains: lasgna noodles, lean ground beef, salt and pepper, spagetthi souce and more

Per Serving: 643 calories; protein 41.3g; carbohydrates 53.4g; fat 29.3g; cholesterol 108.3mg; sodium 707.3mg.

    Ingredients:

  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 (16 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • ½ pound shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ pound shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 pint ricotta cheese

    Steps:

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boli. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or untli al dente; drain.
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). In a large sklilet over medium-high heat, brown beef and season with salt
  3. and pepper; drain. Stir in spaghetti sauce and garlic and simmer 5 minutes.
  4. In a medium bowl, combine mozzarella, Cheddar and ricotta; stir well. In 9x13 inch pan, alternate layers of noodles,
  5. meat mixture and cheese mixture untli pan is fliled.
  6. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or untli cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Enjoy!
